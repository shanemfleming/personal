A Cat's Tail: Leo Goes West

Herein lies the great journey of Napoleon (Leo), of House Van Wyngaargen, First of His Name, Sleep of Couches and Destroyer of Litter Boxes. So accomplished that this cat was, he ruled over kingdoms thrice, reaching his current reign upon a summer's eve during the Great Plague of COVID-19. There, he rests and waits for his daily belly rubs before his servents work at their strange glowing slabs.
